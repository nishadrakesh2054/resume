Frequently asked questions

01 What technologies do you use?

React, Next.js, Node.js, Express, NestJS, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, React Native, Docker, Coolify, Cloudflare, WordPress, and headless CMS platforms.

02 Are you available for freelance or full-time work?

Yes — open to full-time, contract, and freelance work. Based in Kathmandu with remote, hybrid, and on-site options across Nepal and international clients.

03 How many years of experience do you have?

2.5+ years of professional full-stack development — from MERN and PERN web apps and APIs to production deployments, working with startups, agencies, and product teams in Nepal and abroad.

04 What types of projects have you built?

Education portals, medical and hospital sites, sports league platforms, restaurant and hospitality websites, SaaS dashboards, admin panels, and full MERN stack web applications. See the portfolio section for examples.

05 What does your typical project workflow look like?

Scope and requirements → architecture and milestones → build in iterations with check-ins → testing → deploy to staging or production → handoff with documentation. You stay updated at each stage.

06 How long does a project usually take?

It depends on scope. A marketing site or landing page is often 1–2 weeks; an MVP or custom web app commonly 4–8+ weeks; larger platforms are delivered in phases. I share a realistic timeline after understanding your goals and features.

07 Can you work on an existing codebase or only new builds?

Both. I can audit an existing React/Node/MERN project, fix bugs, add features, improve performance and SEO, refactor messy areas, and help your team ship faster — not only greenfield projects.

08 Do you handle deployment, hosting, and DevOps?

Yes. Docker-based setups, Coolify, Cloudflare (DNS, CDN, SSL), environment configuration, and production-ready deployments — plus basic monitoring and performance tuning so the app stays fast and reliable.

09 Do you build mobile apps as well as websites?

Yes — cross-platform mobile with React Native, alongside responsive web apps. Good fit when you want one team for API, admin dashboard, and mobile client.

10 Do you work with international clients and time zones?

Yes. I collaborate remotely with async updates (Slack, email, GitHub) and scheduled video calls when overlap matters. Communication is in English; deliverables follow agreed milestones and deadlines.

11 What should I include in my first message?

A short brief: full-time role vs freelance project, goals, must-have features, target timeline, budget range (for contract work), and any links to designs, repos, or references. The more context you share, the faster I can suggest next steps.
